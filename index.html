About FILTRA

Welcome to Breath Bricks, where innovation meets sustainability to create a healthier, more vibrant world. Based in Lubao, we are pioneers in eco-friendly construction, specializing in our revolutionary "Breath Bricks"—bricks designed to enhance air quality, promote eco-design, and foster vibrant community spaces. Our commitment is to transform the way we build, one breathable brick at a time, focusing on products that support healthier living and stronger communities.

At Breath Bricks, we're not just building bricks—we're building a legacy of sustainability and community. Our team is dedicated to developing high-quality, breathable materials that integrate seamlessly into modern architecture while prioritizing environmental responsibility. Join us in our journey toward a greener future. For more information on our products or partnerships, contact us today.

Why Choose FILTRA?

Eco-Friendly: Sustainable materials and production processes.
Health-Focused: Air purification and quality enhancement.
Durable: Superior strength and longevity.
